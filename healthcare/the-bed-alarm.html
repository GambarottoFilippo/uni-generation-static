Pauns Bed Alarm
Our Bed Alarm System has dramatically reduced falls in all cases where it was implemented.

Our product is the right choice for a caregiver at home, a nursing facility, or hospital needing a quiet, audible and /or visual fall prevention system.

The bed alarm is placed underneath the person at-risk. It is designed to be non-invasive, non slippery and with long lasting battery.

It connects wirelessly to a fall monitor or alarm unit. The warning is activated as soon as the person uplift the whole body from the sensor surface but can always be muted if needed.
Up to 50 beds can be connected to a single monitor point, with a max distance of 200mt.

The software can be adapted to any screen size.
