Human Resources Department Setup
The process of setting up a human resource department will differs based on whether the organization is brand new or if the organization has been in operation for some time as well as the size of the organization.

Many business owners of small organizations start out to be their own HR manager, handling employment issues. Somehow they often missed out policies concerning pay and benefits, compensation, statutory requirements and many more which are important. As the organizations grow, not having a proper set-up can be disastrous.

  • Registration with Statutory Bodies (EPF, SOCSO, IRB, HRDF)
  • HR Documentations (Forms, Policies, Letters, etc)
  • HR Procedures

Human Resource Documents
Accurate & complete documentation allows organization to maintain standards and to allow decisions to be made based on information vailable. It also helps govern the organization with standard rules and regulation that is consistent instead of gut feel.

Human Resource Information System (HRIS)
HRIS is a software that digitize systems and processes to ensure that HR management, business processes and employee data are managed adequately. Among functions available in HRIS are payroll processing, attendance records, recruitment, employee data, etc. While getting the organization ready to implement the state of the art system, a lot of time will be spent of sourcing and implementation. By out-sourcing this service, organization not only cost on their employee’s time but also enable the expert to assist them to do the best. HRIS sourcing & shortlisting of vendors (FREE service).

  • HRIS Implementation consultancy
  • HRIS process re-engineering
